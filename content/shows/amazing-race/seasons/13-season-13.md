Ten teams — a trimmed cast for this outing — left Los Angeles
into a twelve-leg autumn route through Brazil, New Zealand,
Cambodia, India, Kazakhstan, and Russia. CBS held the Sunday
hour and the production leaned into longer legs in fewer
countries. The casting goes deep on family pairings and
partner duos. Phil Keoghan hosts a season the franchise had
earned the audience's trust to make. Quietly assured.
