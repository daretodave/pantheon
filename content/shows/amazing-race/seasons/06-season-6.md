Back to the autumn slot only months after the summer run
wrapped. Eleven teams left Chicago into a twelve-leg route
through Iceland, Scandinavia, Central Europe, and Asia, with
a brisker overall pace than the previous outings. The casting
leans heavily on dating couples and the editing follows the
friction closely. CBS kept the Tuesday hour. Phil Keoghan
hosts a season audibly aware of the franchise's rhythm.
