Eleven returning teams came back for the franchise's second
full all-stars outing, leaving Santa Clarita into a twelve-leg
spring route through China, Malaysia, Sri Lanka, Italy,
Switzerland, Spain, and England before a Las Vegas finish — with
the show's first-ever stop in Wales along the way. CBS held the
Sunday hour and stocked the legs with Express Passes, Double
U-Turns, and Speed Bumps. The casting is the draw: a veteran
field that already knows the format cold.
