The summer experiment. CBS moved the fifth outing into July
and gave Phil Keoghan a thirteen-leg route that ran from
Santa Monica through Uruguay, southern Africa, the Russian
Far East, and back across the Pacific. The casting plays
heavier on long-running romantic partnerships, which gives
the editing room to sit with the friction. The Tuesday slot
built steady summer numbers. The show proves it travels in
any season.
