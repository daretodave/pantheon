The most logistically extraordinary outing the franchise has
run. Filming began with hometown starts in early 2020, then
stopped after three legs through England and Scotland; it
resumed roughly nineteen months later. Some teams couldn't
come back, so two previously-out teams were reinstated, and the
resumed legs ran on a chartered Titan Airways 757 with
staggered releases. The eleven-leg route also touched
Switzerland, France, Greece, and Portugal before a Greater Los
Angeles finish — a season the world interrupted.
