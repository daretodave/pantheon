The race stocked its spring outing with social-media
personalities, each paired with a relative or friend. Eleven
teams started from their own US hometowns — arrivals announced
across their feeds — into a twelve-leg route through Mexico,
Colombia, Switzerland, France, Armenia, Georgia, the UAE,
Indonesia, and China before a Santa Barbara wine-country finish,
with first-ever stops in Colombia, Armenia, and Georgia. CBS
held the Friday hour, and the casting concept reshapes the
on-camera energy from the first front door.
