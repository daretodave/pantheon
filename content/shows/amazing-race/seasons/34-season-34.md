The first season to start the US race outside the United
States. Twelve teams launched from Munich, the widest field
since Season 15, then ran through Austria, Italy, France,
Spain, and Iceland before a Jordan stretch and a Nashville
finish. The production folded in Mega Legs, single legs that
span two episodes rather than one, which stretches the familiar
leg-to-leg rhythm. The international opening and the wider cast
give the early route a texture standard hometown-start seasons
cannot manufacture.
