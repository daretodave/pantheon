The race opened its starting line to other CBS competition
shows for the first time: five Amazing Race teams against three
Survivor and three Big Brother teams. Eleven teams left Hermosa
Beach into a twelve-leg spring route through Japan, Laos,
Vietnam, the UAE, Uganda, Switzerland, Croatia, the
Netherlands, and England before a Detroit finish. CBS held the
Wednesday hour. Phil Keoghan paces a season whose argument is
the strategy clash — three games' worth of instincts tested on
one road.
