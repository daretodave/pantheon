Eleven teams left Los Angeles in November, and the route ran
through Ireland, the Netherlands, Italy, Croatia, Burkina
Faso, and the Taiwan corridor. The writers' strike disrupted
the broader CBS schedule that winter, which compressed the
airing window and tightened the production calendar. The
casting leans toward dating couples and the editing keeps a
brisk twelve-leg cadence. Phil Keoghan works the hand-offs
with the franchise's full muscle memory.
