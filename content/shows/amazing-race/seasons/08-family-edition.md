The season the show tried something different and pulled
back from. Ten teams of four — parents with their kids — ran
an eleven-leg route that stayed almost entirely within the
continental US, with short detours into Mexico, Costa Rica,
and Canada. The four-person format crowded the vans and
slowed the airport-to-airport rhythm the audience had come
to expect. Phil Keoghan hosts with care. The experiment
never returned.
