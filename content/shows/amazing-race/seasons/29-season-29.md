The race ran its boldest casting reset: twenty-two strangers
met and chose their racing partners at the starting line. Eleven new teams left Los Angeles into a twelve-leg
spring route through Panama, Brazil, Tanzania, Norway, Italy,
Greece, Vietnam, and South Korea before a Chicago finish. CBS
held the Friday hour and added a pre-starting-line task, a
reusable U-Turn, and a Roadblock cap that lifts before leg
nine — partnerships built on a hunch and tested from the
first plane.
