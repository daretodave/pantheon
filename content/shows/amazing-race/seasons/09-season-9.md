The reset. After the family experiment, the format snapped
back to eleven two-person teams and a global route — out of
Denver and across Brazil, central Europe, the Mediterranean,
and the Middle East. CBS moved the hour to Wednesday for the
spring. The casting returns to the archetypes the show had
built its audience on, and Phil Keoghan greets pit stops on
four continents. The race feels like the race again.
