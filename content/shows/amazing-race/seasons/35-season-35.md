The largest field the franchise had ever run. Thirteen teams
covered roughly 23,800 miles across three continents and nine
countries, from a Los Angeles start through Thailand, Vietnam,
and India, then Germany, Austria, Slovenia, Sweden, and Ireland
before a Greater Seattle finish. The season moved to
feature-length 90-minute episodes, which gives each leg more
room than the standard hour. Commercial flights, the Express
Pass, and the U-Turn returned, joined by a new Blind U-Turn
vote that sharpens the strategic layer.
