The route left Santa Monica in late spring and ran through
the South Pacific, Southeast Asia, and the Indian subcontinent
before climbing into Western Europe. CBS aired the fourth
outing as a summer-shoulder run, which kept the audience
leaning in week to week. The casting trusts the romantic
pairings the show had been building toward — couples at every
stage of their relationships. Phil Keoghan keeps the pace
honest.
