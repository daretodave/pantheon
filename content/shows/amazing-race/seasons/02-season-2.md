Eleven teams left from the dust of Pahrump, Nevada, and the
route ran across the Mediterranean, the Horn of Africa, and
southern Asia. CBS slotted the second outing into a Monday
hour and let the format breathe for a full thirteen legs. The
casting widens its archetypes — sibling pairs, dating couples,
lifelong friends. Phil Keoghan settles into the throughline.
The hour finds its editing grammar.
