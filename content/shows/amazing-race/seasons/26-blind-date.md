The race ran a casting experiment for its spring outing: five
blind-date pairs met their partners at the starting line,
racing alongside six established couples. Eleven teams left
Castaic Lake into a twelve-leg route through Japan, Thailand,
Germany, France, Monaco, Namibia, the Netherlands, and Peru
before a Dallas finish. CBS held the Friday hour and built Date
Night rewards and a Blind Double U-Turn into the legs, so the
strangers learn each other and the format at the same time.
