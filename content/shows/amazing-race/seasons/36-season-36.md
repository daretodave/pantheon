The all-Americas season. Thirteen teams covered 11,711
miles across two continents and eight countries, from a Puerto
Vallarta start through Mexico, Colombia, Chile, Argentina,
Uruguay, Barbados, and the Dominican Republic before a
Philadelphia finish, with the franchise's first visits to
Barbados and the Dominican Republic. Teams flew on a chartered
Global Crossing Airlines A320 rather than commercial flights,
and the season ran no non-elimination legs. The 90-minute
episodes restored footage the standard hour would have left on
the cutting-room floor.
