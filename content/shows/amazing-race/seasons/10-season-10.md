Twelve teams left Seattle and the route ran through Mongolia,
Vietnam, India, Madagascar, and the Mediterranean before
turning back toward the finish. CBS held the show in the
Sunday hour through the fall, leaning on the post-football
lead-in for steady numbers. The casting widens its
demographic reach noticeably, and the editing trusts longer
legs in unfamiliar geographies. Phil Keoghan paces the pit
stops cleanly. A confident production year.
