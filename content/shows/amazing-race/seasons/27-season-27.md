Eleven teams left Los Angeles into a twelve-leg autumn route
through Brazil, Argentina, Zambia, France, the Netherlands,
Poland, India, Hong Kong, and Macau before finishing in
Southampton — and the show's first-ever stop in Zimbabwe. CBS
held the Friday hour and tightened two tools: the Express Pass
now has to be handed to a rival after use, and the U-Turn moved
onto the Detour decision point. The casting leans on partner
pairs running a competent standard route with sharper mechanics.
