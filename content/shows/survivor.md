Survivor is the reality format every other reality format is
measured against. Twenty-five years and forty-plus seasons in,
it still sets the rules: marooned strangers, a hostile location,
a vote that decides who stays. CBS hands the show summer and
fall on the schedule like a standing reservation. The mother
format hasn't aged so much as accumulated — every twist,
alliance, and idol the genre runs on can be traced back here.
