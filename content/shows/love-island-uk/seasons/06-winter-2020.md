Series 6 is the franchise's big experiment: a winter run, a South African villa, and a new host. The casting energy carries the established summer template into a different season and setting, and the format proves it travels. It does not have the cultural weight of the peak summers, and the transition shows around the edges, but it is a confident, watchable proof of concept that opened a new chapter for the show.
