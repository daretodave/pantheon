The season that split its opening in two. Season 15 brings
the largest premiere cast the franchise has assembled and
spreads it across a two-part premiere, reshaping how the
first stretch of the competition plays out. RuPaul Charles
and Michelle Visage anchor the panel for the first full
MTV-era flagship year. The scale is the story — a huge
lineup with broad range across drag styles. The premiere
structure is the swing; the contest behind it runs solid
and modern.
