A deliberately newbie-driven summer. Sixteen newcomers walk in
and get sorted into four teams on the premiere, picked by team
captains in front of the cast. The High Roller's Room wildcard
and a BB Bucks economy sit on top of the game without owning it.
Widely noted as the most diverse cast Big Brother had assembled
to that point. Thirty episodes, anchored by Julie Chen Moonves.
A season the modern era built by trusting its new players.
