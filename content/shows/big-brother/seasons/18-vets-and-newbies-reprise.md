The season the format revisits its veterans-and-newbies engine, five years after the original run. Four returning houseguests — recognizable faces from the prior decade — slot in alongside twelve newcomers, and the Roadkill twist gives the week a secret third nominator the house has to identify. The Battle Back competition pulls an evicted houseguest back into the game. The texture is sharper than the takeover season that preceded it. A confident reset for the modern era.
