The franchise's second all-star season, and the first since
2006. Sixteen returning houseguests spanning the show's full
history walk back into Studio City, produced inside a strict
bubble. The season is twist-light by design — a single Safety
Suite wrinkle, and otherwise the cast is the format. Every move
lands with seasons of prior context behind it. Julie Chen
Moonves anchors thirty-three episodes. The modern era's argument
that Big Brother has built its own legends, made on screen.
