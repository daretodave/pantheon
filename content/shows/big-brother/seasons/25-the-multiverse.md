The 25th-anniversary season, and the longest run Big Brother
has ever produced — roughly 100 days and around 46 episodes. The
BB Multiverse drops a new weekly universe on the house, each one
a power or a curse, under a Comicverse theme that frames the
whole summer as a milestone. A cast of sixteen to seventeen,
anchored by Julie Chen Moonves. An ambitious anniversary swing,
and the streaming era's anchor text for what modern Big Brother
reaches for.
