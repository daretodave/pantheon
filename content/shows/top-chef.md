Top Chef is the format that proved competitive cooking could
hold an hour without spectacle — a pantry, a clock, a panel.
Twenty-plus seasons in, the show still treats its kitchens like
a craft-school and its eliminations like a workplace decision.
The facade opens here; canon and season blurbs follow on the
content-velocity passes.
